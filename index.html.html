Tech Trends 2023

Digital Identity

Digital identity is a 1 on 1 relationship with a computer or anything digitally. Digital identity is convenient because you don’t need to remember passwords over various devices, also your transactions are safer and there is reduced identity theft. Some consequences are identity theft, cyberbullying, and online harassment. Required components for digital identity are your full official name, date of birth, place of birth, home address, and a government issued identity number. Some careers related to this trend are Digital identity analysists, Digital identity managers, and Digital identity sales engineers. A website of value for digital identity is ForgeRock.

Quantam Computing

Quantum computing, unlike classical computing, is built on Qubits rather than bits. While bits can store a value of one or zero, qubits can store both in any combination simultaneously this is called a “superposition.” A single calculation leads to a single path in classical computing, but quantum computing can explore a massive number of different paths at the same time due to the volatility of the qubit. Quantum Computers can solve problems significantly faster than classical computers. In 2019, Google announced that a quantum computer solved a problem in 200 seconds (about 3 and a half minutes) that would take a normal computer 10, 000 years. The vast number of solutions it can come up with can make it easier to develop models of molecules in the human body, accelerate the training of AI system, and do prime factorization. This can help the pharmaceutical companies develop drugs, help the automotive companies teach self-driving cars how to make decisions in crucial moments, and factorize large prime numbers that businesses use as the basis for their encryption efforts. One of the main concerns with quantum computing is cybersecurity, especially in the event of large-scale quantum adoption. This means in the geopolitical space whoever gets their first has a huge advantage. Some quantum computers implement qubits using electronic states of charged atoms called ions. Trapped ion-based systems apply quantum gates using lasers to manipulate the electronic state of the ion. Some careers are, Quantum Network Security Engineer, Quantum Systems Technician, Quantum Mechanics Developer, and Quantum Hardware/Software Engineer.

Cloud Computing

Simply put, Cloud computing is storing and accessing data and computing services over the internet rather than using local hard drives and personal data centers. The benefits to society from using Cloud Computing is getting their applications to market quickly. There are, or rather, lack thereof secure API’s. It is easy to access without authentication, which leads to vulnerability. The technology required for Cloud Computing is Client Infrastructure, Application, Service, Runtime Cloud, Storage, Infrastructure, Management, Security, and Internet. Some careers related to cloud computing are cloud administrator, cloud support engineer, cloud security analyst, cloud network engineer, cloud software engineer, and cloud automation engineer. Electrical Vehicals

Electric Vehicle also known as EV is a vehicle that can be powered by an electric motor which brings electricity from a battery and is capable of being charged from an external source. Using EV’s addresses an important public health hazard. They can also lower vehicle fueling costs by half, or more. EV battery production can adversely impact the environment, such as a loss of biodiversity, air pollution and decreased freshwater supply. Some tech Components for this are, Head units, ECU, Electric Drivetrain, Electric Transmission, Motors, and Lithium Batterys.

Virtual Reality

AR- Augmented reality is an enhanced reality that uses text, graphics, and audio along with other enhanced integrated real- time objects. VR- Virtual reality is the use of computer modeling and simulation that allows a person to interact with an artifact that is three dimensional in a sensory environment. AR allows Real-Time applications some society benefits are, AR Enhances Interactive learning, AR Increases of users' engagement, AR allows the Ability to provide a unique experience, VR allows Revolutionary Healthcare, VR boosts Creativity, and VR helps understand complex concepts. Concerns with VR are the headaches, nausea, and eyestrains caused when using for too long. Concerns with AR are the possibilities of physical harm, security and privacy issues, social issues. For AR you need a processor, input devices, sensors, & a display. For VR you need software, headset, audio system, input devices, sensors, processors. Some careers for this are, Virtual Reality Engineer Visual Systems Designer, AR/VR Camera Driver Engineer, AR/VR System Software Engineer, and a Performance Engineer.
